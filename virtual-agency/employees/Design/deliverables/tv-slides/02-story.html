Our story

It started with Russell
“Sprouts” Efros

Russell was a Brooklyn musician, artist, and filmmaker with a gift for making everyone around him feel they belonged. He also struggled with loneliness. In 2018, at 25, he died of fentanyl poisoning, in the middle of building the very thing Sprout is today. His family started the Russell J. Efros Foundation to carry his vision forward, and that foundation grew into Sprout Society.
