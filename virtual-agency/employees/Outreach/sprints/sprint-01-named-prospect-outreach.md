## 6. Rules that govern every message

Every draft in this sprint already follows these. They also govern any edit Max makes before sending.

1. **Never fabricate.** Every `[bracket]` is a real-detail slot. Fill it from the actual post. Never invent a post, a mural, a song, a caption, or a shared connection.
2. **No em dashes.** Restructure with a colon, a period, or a reordered clause. Never swap in a comma.
3. **Active voice, short sentences, one idea each.**
4. **Warm, peer-to-peer, human.** The message sounds like a person, not an org. Never nonprofit-formal, never salesy.
5. **No hype words, no superlatives.** Specific beats grand.
6. **Consistent with the foundational language.** Canonical mission: harnessing the power of the arts to end the loneliness epidemic in creative communities. Throughline: no one builds a creative life alone. Never invent a stat beyond the verified list; flag it for Max instead.
7. **Ambient on Instagram.** Comment before DM. The first DM extends a conversation, it is not a mission paragraph or a big ask.
