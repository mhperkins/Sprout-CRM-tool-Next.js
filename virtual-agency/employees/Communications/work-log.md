## 2026-06-03 — Instagram caption proof: first Sprout n Tell

**Ask (Max):** Proof an Instagram caption recapping the first Sprout n Tell, then show a few alternatives.

**Delivered:**
- Proofed the original caption. Flagged: "HUGE success!" (hype + all-caps), "amazing" (hype word), stiff "if/then" CTA, "Sprout n Tell's" (wrong apostrophe → "Sprout n Tells"), "signup" → "sign up". No em dashes present.
- Delivered a clean revised caption (recap + thank-you + gentle RSVP CTA, fourth-Friday detail).
- Produced 3 alternates on request: short/punchy, warm/reflective, recurring-detail-first.

**Outcome:** Max selected the **first revised caption** (recap + thank-you angle) as the final. Ready to post.

**Selected copy:**
> Our first Sprout n Tell brought artists, musicians, and the whole Sprout Society community into one room. Thank you for sharing your work, and for showing up for each other with so much love ❤️
>
> We do this the fourth Friday of every month. Want to come hang or showcase your own work next time? The link in our bio has everything you need to RSVP or sign up. We'd love to see you there.

**Why the selected version beat the alternates (style signal for training):**
- It **recapped the real event**. The 3 alternates described Sprout n Tell generically; the winner spoke to the specific night that happened.
- It **led with a concrete, physical image** ("into one room") instead of abstract praise.
- Its **gratitude named real actions** ("sharing your work," "showing up for each other"), not generalized thanks.
- The **CTA was woven into the warmth**, not bolted on as a separate "if you're interested" block.

These were promoted into the system prompt's Sprout Voice section (retro loop) so they shape future copy, not just sit here.

**Note:** Instagram captions are normally the Social Media agent's domain. Comms proofed this one at Max's request as owner of Sprout's outbound voice.
