# Monthly Sprout — Newsletter Intake

> **What this is:** the fill-in form for one issue of the **Monthly Sprout** roundup (the compact template). Every section below maps to a real block in the newsletter.
>
> **How to use:**
> 1. Copy this file to `briefs/newsletter-<month>.md` (e.g. `briefs/newsletter-2026-07.md`).
> 2. Fill in what you know. **Leave anything you don't know blank.** Do not guess.
> 3. Tell the Communications Manager the path. It writes the first draft from this.
>
> **The contract with the agent:** it fills every section it can from this intake + the CRM. Anything you left blank, it leaves blank and **flags in a "couldn't fill" list** at the end. It never invents a recap, a name, a date, or a quote. Blank is a valid answer.
>
> Section recaps can be captured the long way first using `docs/newsletter/event-recap-template.md` (the SnT / event recap form); the one-line **blurb** from that form drops straight into the Featured or Past blocks below.
