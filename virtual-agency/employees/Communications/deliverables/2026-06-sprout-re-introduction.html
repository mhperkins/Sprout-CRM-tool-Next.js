Dear Sprout community,

Most of you know me as Russell's sister. Eight years ago, we lost him at just 25 years old.

After nearly a decade practicing law, I've stepped away to lead Sprout Society full time, as its new CEO. I’m doing it unpaid, so every dollar goes straight to impact.

Russell had a gift for making everyone around him feel they belonged. A Brooklyn-based musician, artist, and filmmaker, he had a vision for the very thing Sprout is today.

Sprout Society builds the physical and emotional infrastructure of belonging, for creatives and those who build community around them. That means the spaces, networks, and resources they need to thrive, in their careers and in their lives.

Loneliness is a public health epidemic, and for independent creatives, isolation is built into the work itself: solitary, precarious, and run on unpredictable hours, without the stability most careers provide. It weighs on mental health, and it cuts people off from the opportunities connection brings: advice, collaborators, the economic and career openings that move through a network they don't have. Connection is the medicine.

Because of you, we've served more than 5,000 people, built 50+ programs, raised $1.7 million, and kept every single service at our Bushwick hub donation-based.

We don't just serve this community. We invest in it. Through the Sprout Fund, we've directed over $100,000 to 32 creatives' projects: seed support for the creative work that brings communities together.

You showed up for our family and for Russell, and so many lives are different because of it.

Here's how we're growing that vision now:

  • Membership is live. Members get access to our programming, dedicated space, and the ability to host their own community programming at Sprout. It turns Sprout from a place people attend into a community people build.
  • A digital community. An online channel where creatives find each other beyond the space, free and open to all.
  • Sprout Fund. We back creatives financially, not just with space. The fund seeds the projects that bring communities together through and around creativity, and we're growing it to fund more of them.
  • The Scholarship Fund. Our promise that cost will never keep someone out. A gift here is a person welcomed in who wouldn't be here without you.
  • An updated website with a live calendar. Recommended events, shows, and gatherings, all in one accessible place.
If it's been a while since you heard from us, that's about to change. You'll be hearing from us regularly now. Real updates, real stories, and upcoming events you can be part of. The first one up will be our monthly newsletter.

Our vision of a creative life that's connected, supported, and full of opportunity is what we're growing, together. This is the most important, most pivotal year in Sprout's life yet.

Join us in continuing to grow it. Give what you can, come see the space, bring someone who needs it.

Thank you for being part of it.

With love and gratitude,
Danielle & The Efros Family
