  Community Sprout Shout Pat Hopkins aka Proactive Panic
Proactive Panic is a genre-smashing electronic producer from Brooklyn blending melodic harmonies and heavy beats across the BPM spectrum. He’s dropping a new dubstep single on June 12 and releasing a groovy, ambient melodic EP, ‘Letters In-Between,’ in September. You can find Proactive Panic on Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, and on Instagram at @proactive.panic.
“I was really stunned by how incredibly Sprout Society pulled together our showcase. Not only was the space incredible and the artists and crowd warm, but the organizers were so beyond diligent in communicating and facilitating, making sure all of our needs were met with great care. I’m really excited to keep working with them, they are a very, very special community.”
