The recap

Our first-ever Sprout n Tell packed the room with the best of Bushwick. Live sets from This Here Giraffe, Lucas James McCain, and Normal Again traded off with DJ sets by Cody Naka and Proactive Panic, while art from Potters in Crime, Rachel Weiswasser, and Isabella Manrique lined the walls and Bob the Photo Man captured the night in his antique photo booth. Thank you to everyone who shared something, and to everyone who showed up to soak it in. This is exactly the community we hoped would sprout. See you at Vol. 2.
