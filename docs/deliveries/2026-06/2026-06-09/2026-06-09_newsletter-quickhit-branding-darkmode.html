🎨 Quick Hit = branded header + footer

The single-topic template now matches the Monthly Roundup: black header with the white logo on the left, and the full branded footer (brand line, IG, Donate + Become-a-member buttons, white logo on the right). Killed the old dead [WEBSITE] / Mailchimp merge tags.

🔗 Real Givebutter links

Footer buttons hardcoded to live URLs — Donate → sproutspacedonors (slug pulled from the Givebutter API), Become a member → sproutmembership. Both work out of the box.

📱 Buttons stacked for mobile

Donate / membership now sit top & bottom instead of side by side — in both templates. The compact footer IIFE renders each button as its own row with a 10px spacer.

🎯 Centered labels

Both buttons are a uniform 220px wide with centered text (display:block; text-align:center) — no more uneven shrink-to-text widths.

🌙 Dark-mode accommodation

Added color-scheme + supported-color-schemes meta tags (plus a :root hint, and x-apple-disable-message-reformatting to Quick Hit) to both templates. This stops Apple Mail from force-inverting the black header/footer in dark mode. Honest caveat: Gmail/Outlook ignore these and may still invert — no per-recipient override exists.
