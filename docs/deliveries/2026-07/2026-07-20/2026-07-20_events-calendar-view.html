What changed

🗓 Month-grid calendar

Events now sit on a month grid instead of a list. 🗓 Calendar / ☰ List toggle in the toolbar, defaults to Calendar and remembers your choice. ← → month nav + Today; search & status filters still apply.

🔁 Recurring events expand

New occurrencesInRange() helper places every occurrence in the visible month, not just the next one — respects weekday & until. Chips show start time, a 🔁 marker, and are color-coded by status.

💬 Click-popover

Clicking a chip opens a popover (like the checklist calendar): change the date of a one-time event, View event page →, or Edit. Recurring events show their series summary instead.

✋ Drag to move

Drag a one-time event to another day to change its date; it persists. Recurring events aren't draggable (would shift the series). Drag payload set in onDragStart so it works in Safari too.
