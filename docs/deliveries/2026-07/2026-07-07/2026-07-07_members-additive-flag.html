Members is now an additive flag, not a separate bucket

A contact can be a Member AND stay in their Community / Donor / Prospect bucket

The ask: “any contact can be added to the member bucket… when someone is moved to the member bucket, they stay in their previous bucket as well.” Membership is now a checkbox that stacks on top of the base bucket, not a fourth exclusive tab.
