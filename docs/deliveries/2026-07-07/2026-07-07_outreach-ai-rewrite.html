AI Rewrite for Outreach Messages

Click any outreach text block, type a plain-English instruction ("rewrite this as an invitation to our next Sprout N Tell"), and Claude rewrites it in Sprout's voice. Runs on your own Anthropic account — first-party Claude, not a third party or a watered-down model.

Effort: Medium Model: Sonnet 5 Build ✓ Passing Branch: main
🤖

Your Claude, not an outside one

Same server-side pattern as the newsletter Polish button: official Anthropic SDK, your ANTHROPIC_API_KEY, key never in the browser.

🧠

Sonnet 5, stronger than Polish

Deliberately upgraded from Polish's Haiku 4.5 to Sonnet 5 for nuanced rewrites, with room for a few short paragraphs.

↶

Non-destructive

The rewrite lands in the editor, not on disk. Undo restores your original. A bad rewrite never eats your message.

🗓️

Knows the real dates

Fed your live upcoming events, so an invitation uses the actual Sprout N Tell date. Missing details become a [bracketed] placeholder, never invented.

How you use it

1

Open the Outreach page and click a text block to edit it.

2

A ✨ panel appears underneath with an instruction box.

3

Type what you want and press Enter or Rewrite.

4

Edit the result, click away to save, or ↶ Undo / Esc to discard.
